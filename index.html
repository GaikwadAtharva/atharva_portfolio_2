My Approach

01

I don't reach for frameworks before understanding the problem. Pointers before libraries, algorithms before abstractions. A strong base lets you build anything and debug everything.

Every concept gets turned into something tangible — a script, a tool, a small program. Reading is a starting point; building is where understanding actually sticks.

Talent is unreliable. Discipline isn't. I show up every day — breakthrough session or twenty minutes of dry practice. Small consistent effort is the only reliable strategy.
